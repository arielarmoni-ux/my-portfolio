Ariel Armoni | Exhibition Design & Architecture

I am an architect and exhibition designer specializing in translating curatorial visions into design concepts that are inherently tied to an exhibition’s narrative. My process bridges the gap between abstract ideas and physical space, ensuring that content and form resonate as a single, cohesive entity.

To achieve this, I utilize advanced digital tools and high-fidelity 3D visualizations, providing a profound understanding of the exhibition's atmosphere and flow long before it is built. Beyond spatial design, I collaborate closely with artists to develop and realize their works, bringing a meticulous architectural perspective to the creative process.

My practice spans a diverse range of environments, from contemporary art and archaeology to interactive exhibitions for children.
